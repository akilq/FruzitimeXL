About FRUZITIME RADIO

More than 10 years ago, Akil Quirindongo had a hobby that turned into a vision..

During the online music sharing boom of the early 2000s, he thought of a way to bring Caribbean music, "Ritmo Kombina", into the homes of people who were missing their islands.

At the same time, he wondered if he could pull off other music genres as well. He could, and he did.
